A bright white point light is placed at the center of the scene, but because lights are not directly viewable, a white matte sphere of maximum intensity is placed at the scene center, giving the illusion that the light comes from the sphere. Note that there are no shadows.

TextureMaps

The texture maps below are low resolution versions of some wonderful images that can be found on the Web. Sources are listed where known.
  • NASA's Blue Marble data sets: (http://earthobservatory.nasa.gov/Newsroom/BlueMarble)
    • earth1.ppm
    • earth2.ppm
    • earth3.ppm
  • Clementine moon mission: (http://astrogeology.usgs.gov/Projects/Clementine/index.html)
    • moon.pgm
  • James Hastings-Trew: (http://gw.marketingden.com/planets/planets.html)
    • mars.ppm
    • jupiter.ppm
  • Bjorn Jonsson: (http://ww.mmedia.is/~bjj) - some excellent planetary texture maps that can be freely used but not redistributed here.
